About : Loisingha College, Loisingha, Balangir

The college was established in 1983 with teaching facilities in IA And +2 Arts. The college is known as Loisingha College Loisingha Dist-Balangir. In the year 1989 +3 Arts(Pass) opened with 64 seats. In 1990-91 the sanctioned strength in +2 Arts increased from 128 to 256 and -2 science stream opened with 128 seats. The aim of the college is to provide Higher Education to Socially, Educationally undeveloped people of the rural area.
